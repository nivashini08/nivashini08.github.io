My story
My
Journey intoDesign

My journey into design began with a curiosity about how digital experiences are created and how users interact with them. As a Computer Science student, I initially focused on functionality, but exploring UI/UX helped me understand the role of visuals in shaping user perception.
I realized that design is not just about aesthetics—it’s about communicating ideas clearly. This led me to start creating social media creatives and UI layouts in Figma, where I learned the importance of structure, typography, and consistency.

"I design with intent—every visual should communicate, not just decorate."

Through my internships, I gained practical insights into design. At Reccsar, I learned to design for users with limited digital experience, focusing on real accessibility. At Tekfilo, I understood how clarity and structure— even in documentation—are essential parts of good design.

I speak Tamil natively, English professionally, and Japanese at an elementary level — and I bring that multicultural lens to every visual system I build. I'm actively seeking a creative or graphic design internship where I can contribute, learn, and grow.
